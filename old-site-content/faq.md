## Building & Construction FAQs

### Q: What qualifications are needed for a mortgage?

**A**: Requirements include:
- Historical (3-5 years) Hopewell financial information
- 25% initial cash outlay
- Pledge campaign commitments
- Property assessments
- Contractor quotes
- 60-day processing period
- South Carolina UMC Conference approvals

---

### Q: What are the anticipated costs?

**A**: The sanctuary project is estimated at **$2,600,000**

This includes:
- Supporting infrastructure requirements
- Grading
- Stormwater management
- Additional parking
- Utility upgrades

---

### Q: When will construction begin?

**A**: The congregation can break ground **three and a half to four months** after raising the necessary funds for a down payment on a mortgage.

---

### Q: What monthly giving is required?

**A**: Using example calculations:
- **Project Cost**: $2,600,000
- **Down Payment**: $500,000
- **Interest Rate**: 6%
- **Term**: 20 years
- **Estimated Monthly Payment**: ~$12,000

*Note: Final amount may change based on loan provider discussions*

## Property & Facilities

### Q: Will historic features be preserved?

**A**:
- **Stone Wall**: Should remain unaffected
- **Oak Trees**: Will be preserved with every effort
- **Picnic Shelter**: Stays in current location
- **Playground**: Stays in current location
- **Brush Arbor**: Will likely be relocated or removed

## Worship & Services

### Q: Why not multiple Sunday services?

**A**: The congregation survey showed most members preferred maintaining one service. Adding multiple services would strain:
- Musicians
- Hospitality team
- Worship committees

---

### Q: About classroom spaces?

**A**: Current worship space will address remaining classroom needs until completing two additional building phases.
